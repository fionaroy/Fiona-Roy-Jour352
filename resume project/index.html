My name is Fiona Roy (she/her/hers) and I am a current journalism student at the University of Maryland, College Park.

My current focus is to become an investigative journliast that focuses on climate and environmental issues. I wish to be able to provide the public easy-to-read synopsis' of scientific findings to make the knowledge easier to understand for a wider-range of consumers. My goal is to increase awareness and inspire action to prevent the worsening of climate change to help protect both people and the Earth.

From previous jobs and leadership positions, I have gained lots of experience in high-stress, fast-paced situations which have helped develop strong multi-tasking, leadership and teamwork skills.

I hope to one day create a a news-source that people look to with confidence and trust.
